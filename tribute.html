Sundar Pichai


Chief Executive Officer of Alphabet
Here is the timeline of Pichai's Life
  • 1972: Born as Pichai Sundararajan in Madras(now Chennai),India
  • 2004: Joined Google, led Product management and innovation efforts for a Google's Client software products, including Google Chrome Web Browser and Chrome OS. Also went overseas for Google Maps Deployement.
  • 2009: Pichai gave demonstraion of Chrome OS.
  • 2010:Announced of the new video codec VP8 by Google and introduced new video format , WebM.
  • 2011: Chromebook Released for Trail and Testing.
  • 2012: Chromebook Released to Public.
  • 2013: Added android to list of Google Products which was formerly managed by Andy Rubin.
  • 2015: He was selected and became the CEO(Chief Executive Officer) of Google on August 10. Later in October He stepped into new position at the completion of formation of Alphabet Inc. the new holding company of Google Company family.
  • 2017: Pichai was a speaker at the World Internet Conference in China.
  • 2019: Pichai became the CEO of Alphabet Inc.
  • 2020: Pichai delivered a speech virtually at the Singapore FinTech Festival emphasizing inclusive digital economy.
